SOLUTION

Our solution is to cut down alluminium wastage by having garbage bins in the public that has on option to recycle drinking cans on the go. These bins will have a separate hole where you can slide the cans in. The cans will quickly be melted, and aluminum ingots could be used later.

The garbage bin scans and recycles the aluminium cans in separate holes, and then melts the can. The bin will process what type of material it is, and then go on the further steps which is to place the material in the right chamber. It will not loose any quality, and you can repeat the process over and over again!
